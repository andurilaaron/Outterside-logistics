Domestic Freight Services

Domestic freight for cargo that needs clear pickup control, delivery evidence, route discipline and practical communication from booking to close-out.

Controlled domestic movement

Not just freight booking. Movement control.

Domestic freight fails when the cargo is poorly defined, the receiver is not ready, the wrong vehicle arrives, the driver has no site instructions, or nobody owns the handover. Outterside Logistics structures the movement before the truck is dispatched.

We support metro, regional and interstate freight with a focus on reliable coordination, accurate cargo data, documented handover and practical escalation when the plan changes.

Best suited for

  • Business-to-business freight
  • High-value equipment movements
  • Palletised and project cargo
  • Urgent site-to-site freight
  • Controlled freight requiring delivery evidence
  • Freight needing receiver coordination

Movement control process

1

Confirm cargo data

Description, piece count, gross weight, dimensions, packaging condition and special handling requirements.

2

Confirm route and equipment

Vehicle type, loading method, access restrictions, timing window, site contacts and delivery constraints.

3

Execute and track

Pickup confirmation, transit communication, delivery confirmation and exception escalation if required.

4

Close out

Proof of delivery, invoice reference, incident notes and any repeat-lane improvements.
