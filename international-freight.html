International Freight

Air and sea freight coordination for businesses that need cleaner documentation, stronger forwarder instructions and fewer surprises at the border.

Cross-border movement control

International freight starts with clean cargo data.

Most international freight problems begin before the shipment leaves: weak descriptions, missing values, incorrect weights, poor packing data, unclear Incoterms, missing permits or vague forwarder instructions.

Outterside Logistics helps structure the shipment before it enters the forwarder, airline, shipping line or customs pathway.

Support can include

  • Air freight coordination
  • Sea freight coordination
  • Commercial invoice and packing list support
  • Shipper letter of instruction support
  • Forwarder quote comparison
  • Milestone and exception tracking
  • Export and import documentation packs

Documentation discipline

DocumentPurposeWhy it matters
Commercial InvoiceDeclares parties, description, value, origin and transaction details.Supports customs valuation and clearance.
Packing ListDefines piece count, dimensions, weights and package detail.Prevents handling, booking and clearance errors.
SLI / Forwarder InstructionGives movement instructions to the forwarder.Reduces assumptions around route, service level and documentation.
Delivery DocketRecords local pickup or delivery handover.Creates evidence for the local movement leg.
